Federal Employees

If you work for a bank, airline, telecom company, railway, or interprovincial transport company, you are likely covered by the Canada Labour Code — not your provincial legislation. Federal employees with 12+ months of service have unjust dismissal protection, and common law notice can be substantial. Start the calculator and select "Federal Employee" at the province step.

Why Province Matters

ESA Minimums Vary

Different rules in every province

Ontario employees can receive up to 34 weeks (8 termination + 26 severance). Newfoundland employees are capped at 6 weeks. The variation is significant — this is why province-specific information matters.

Common Law is National

Bardal factors apply everywhere

Common law notice is based on Canadian case law (Bardal v. Globe & Mail, 1960) and applies in every province and territory. Courts across Canada use the same factors: age, years of service, position, and job availability.
